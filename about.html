About Natasha Fredericks

AI Advocate. Digital Systems Strategist. Builder of practical AI capability across Africa.

The Vision

Africa is not behind. Africa is under enabled. My mission is to make practical artificial intelligence accessible, understandable, and usable across the continent. Not as hype. Not as theory. But as a daily productivity tool that increases opportunity.

The Founder

With over a decade of experience in executive operations, coordination, and digital systems, I understand how real organisations function. I have worked in high pressure environments, supported leadership teams, and built structured systems that keep operations moving.

That foundation allows me to approach AI differently. Not from a purely technical angle, but from a practical implementation perspective. If a tool cannot fit into the workflow of a small business, an NGO, or a corporate team, it will not be adopted. Adoption is where transformation happens.

The Initiative

Through Project2Pixel, I am building a continent focused AI enablement initiative. The goal is clear. Enable 50,000 Africans with practical AI skills and structured adoption frameworks.

This work focuses on awareness, responsible use, adoption support, and scalable partnership models. It is designed to work within real world constraints such as bandwidth limitations, budget sensitivity, and varying digital literacy levels.

Why It Matters

Artificial intelligence will shape productivity, employment, and economic participation across every sector. Africa must not be a passive consumer of global AI systems. We must be active users, builders, trainers, and decision makers.

This is not just about tools. It is about capability. It is about confidence. It is about ensuring that AI becomes a lever for growth rather than a source of fear.
