About Hephzibah Foundation

Reviving Hope. Restoring Value.

We exist to help people move from crisis to stability — and from survival mode to dignity.

“Hephzibah” means My delight is in her — a declaration of restoration, transformation, and renewed identity. It represents a turning point: from brokenness to rebuilding, from struggle to strength, and from despair to dignity. That’s the heart of our work: practical compassion that creates real change.

Our Impact Goal

Serving 50 000 lives through restoration

We are building initiatives designed to reach and support 50 000 people through safe housing, food security, education support, wellness programmes, and skills development. This isn’t a “nice-to-have” goal — it’s the scale we’re designing for.

Short Term

Immediate relief: food support, basic essentials, crisis response, and safe shelter access.

Medium Term

Stability: ongoing family support, education assistance, structured wellness programmes.

Long Term

Transformation: skills, employability pathways, stronger households, stronger communities.

Our Mission

Mission for sustainable change

To restore hope and create sustainable transformation through housing initiatives, education support, and wellness-driven programmes that rebuild lives and renew purpose.

Our Vision

Communities that thrive

A South Africa where people rise from hardship into healing — restored, empowered, and recognised as valuable.

Our Core Pillars

How we work

Restoration — Safe housing and essential support to rebuild stability.
Empowerment — Skills, mentorship, and education support to break cycles of poverty.
Compassion in Action — Practical support that meets real needs with real outcomes.
Transformation — Programmes that heal, uplift, and create long-term change.
