Natasha Fredericks

Public AI advocate and founder of Project2Pixel. Advancing structured artificial intelligence capability across Africa and globally.

Public Voice and Advocacy

Natasha Fredericks is a continental artificial intelligence advocate focused on practical capability, responsible adoption and measurable implementation. Her work centres on moving artificial intelligence conversations beyond hype and into structured application across institutions, communities and emerging economies.

Through her role as a Microsoft Elevate Her Ambassador, Natasha contributed to public conversations on AI readiness, digital inclusion and responsible technology adoption. She created educational content, engaged diverse audiences and participated in discussions focused on expanding access to artificial intelligence literacy.

This exposure revealed a critical gap. Awareness alone does not create transformation. Capability, structure and long term implementation do.

From Operations to AI Systems

With a foundation in executive level operations and systems coordination, Natasha brings implementation discipline into the AI space. Her transition into full stack development training strengthened her understanding of digital architecture, infrastructure and scalable technology systems.

This technical grounding informs her advocacy. Artificial intelligence is not only about prompts or tools. It is about systems thinking, governance, integration and sustainable usage at scale.

Training and Capacity Building

Training is central to the Project2Pixel model. Natasha designs and delivers structured artificial intelligence capability programmes for corporates, institutions, youth networks and emerging leaders.

Programmes are built around practical application, responsible adoption and measurable implementation. Each engagement moves participants from awareness into structured usage that strengthens systems and improves productivity.

  • Corporate AI readiness workshops
  • Executive and leadership briefings
  • Youth AI literacy and emerging talent bootcamps
  • Institutional adoption frameworks
  • AI productivity and workflow masterclasses

Founder of Project2Pixel

Project2Pixel was established as a structured response to the artificial intelligence capability gap. What began as digital execution evolved into a continental AI enablement initiative with global relevance.

The initiative focuses on public advocacy, structured training and partnership driven scale to expand responsible AI capability across sectors and regions.

Speaking and Engagement Themes

  • AI readiness in emerging markets
  • Responsible AI adoption frameworks
  • Women in artificial intelligence and digital inclusion
  • Youth AI capability development pipelines
  • Moving from awareness to implementation

Mission

To advance structured artificial intelligence capability through public advocacy, measurable training programmes and responsible adoption frameworks that strengthen institutions and expand opportunity globally.

Vision

A world where artificial intelligence capability is not confined to a few regions, but expanded through structured education, responsible implementation and globally competitive systems.
