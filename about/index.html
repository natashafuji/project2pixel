Natasha Fredericks

Founder of Project2Pixel — helping SMEs, NGOs and organisations use websites, AI, automation and digital systems in practical ways.

Bridging the gap between digital tools and everyday business operations.

Founder Story

Project2Pixel was founded by Natasha Fredericks, whose professional foundation was built in executive administration, production coordination and high-responsibility operational environments.

After more than a decade working across scheduling, coordination, stakeholder communication, logistics and project support, Natasha recognised a pattern: many organisations do not fail because they lack vision. They struggle because their systems are not built to support growth.

Why Project2Pixel Exists

Project2Pixel exists to help SMEs, NGOs, startups, education initiatives and organisations move from scattered processes into practical digital systems. We build websites, workflows, automation support, training and operational tools that make daily work easier to manage.

The business was shaped by Natasha’s transition into technology and AI, including her involvement as a Microsoft Elevate Her Ambassador. This reinforced the need for accessible, responsible and implementation-focused digital support for organisations that do not have large technical teams.

Our Edge

Project2Pixel sits at the intersection of administration, project management and practical technology. That combination matters because AI and automation are only useful when they solve real operational problems.

Clients do not need to be tech experts to work with us. We understand the messy middle: inboxes, spreadsheets, missed follow-ups, manual admin, unclear processes and overloaded founders. Our role is to translate that chaos into practical systems that teams can actually use.

What We Are Building

Project2Pixel is building a practical digital enablement model for small businesses, NGOs, education initiatives and public-sector aligned organisations. The focus is accessibility, implementation support and real operational improvement — from better websites and forms to clearer workflows, communication systems and AI-supported admin.

The goal is simple: help organisations keep up with where technology is going without needing expensive teams, complex systems or technical overwhelm.

Founder-Led, Impact-Focused

Natasha’s work is driven by a belief that technology should not only serve well-funded organisations. It should also reach the small business owner, the grassroots NGO, the community builder and the young person trying to participate in the digital economy.

Project2Pixel is being built to serve both commercial growth and social impact — because the future of technology should not be reserved for the already connected.
