Africa is not catching up to AI.
Africa is building with it.

Project2Pixel is a continental AI enablement initiative focused on building practical capability across Africa. Not theory. Not trends. Real tools. Real implementation. Real outcomes.

Explore the Initiative Partner with Us

The Opportunity

Artificial intelligence will reshape how economies function, how institutions operate and how people work. The question is not whether Africa participates. The question is whether we participate with capability and confidence.

Our Focus

Capability

Build AI literacy that translates into practical application. Training that leads to usage. Usage that leads to measurable impact.

Responsible Adoption

Embed structure, governance and ethical guardrails into AI usage. Build confidence alongside innovation.

Continental Scale

Partner with corporates, institutions and community organisations to expand reach across regions, sectors and languages.

The Vision

A continent where AI literacy is common, implementation is responsible, and innovation is locally driven. Where African institutions modernise confidently and African entrepreneurs compete globally.
