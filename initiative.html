The Project2Pixel AI Africa Initiative

Building practical AI capability across Africa through education, adoption support, and partnerships.

Partner with Project2Pixel Request a Session

The mission

Enable 50,000 Africans with practical AI skills and responsible adoption support. The focus is real world impact. Less hype. More capability.

The framework

Awareness

Demystify AI and build confidence for beginners.

  • Community sessions
  • Public talks
  • Beginner AI literacy
  • Responsible use principles

Adoption

Help teams apply AI in workflows with guardrails and templates.

  • AI readiness assessment
  • Team bootcamps
  • Prompt libraries by role
  • Templates and playbooks

Acceleration

Scale impact through cohorts, partners, and train the trainer models.

  • Regional cohorts
  • Corporate CSI enablement
  • NGO adoption support
  • Train the trainer programmes

Who this is for

  • Entrepreneurs and small businesses
  • NGOs and community organisations
  • Corporate teams seeking responsible adoption
  • Youth and early career professionals
  • Training partners and ecosystem builders
