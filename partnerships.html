Partnerships

Project2Pixel partners with organisations that want measurable, responsible AI enablement across Africa. This is designed for scale, credibility, and long term impact.

Partnership tracks

Corporate and CSI partnerships

Enable communities, youth, and small businesses through funded programmes.

  • Co branded workshops and cohorts
  • Impact reporting and metrics
  • Train the trainer rollout

NGO and community partners

Equip teams and beneficiaries with practical AI capability.

  • AI literacy sessions
  • Adoption support for operations
  • Templates for reporting and communication

Institutional and training partners

Scale delivery through partners and local facilitators.

  • Facilitator toolkits
  • Cohort rollout support
  • Quality and consistency framework

What we measure

  • Number of people trained
  • Confidence increase from baseline to completion
  • Productivity improvements and time saved
  • Adoption consistency through templates and workflows
  • Programme reach by region and community
